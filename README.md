## Major Stablecoins

Widely used stablecoins backed by fiat reserves or equivalent assets.

- [Tether (USDT)](https://tether.to/) — Fiat-backed stablecoin widely used for trading and liquidity across exchanges.
- [USD Coin (USDC)](https://www.circle.com/usdc) — Fully reserved stablecoin issued by Circle with regular attestations.
- [PayPal USD (PYUSD)](https://www.paypal.com/pyusd) — USD-backed stablecoin integrated into PayPal’s ecosystem.
- [TrueUSD (TUSD)](https://tusd.io/) — Fiat-backed stablecoin with third-party attestations of reserves.
- [Pax Dollar (USDP)](https://paxos.com/usdp/) — Regulated stablecoin issued by Paxos.

## Decentralized & Crypto-Backed Stablecoins

Stablecoins backed by crypto collateral and managed via smart contracts.

- [DAI](https://makerdao.com/en/dai/) — Decentralized stablecoin backed by crypto collateral via MakerDAO.
- [Liquity USD (LUSD)](https://www.liquity.org/) — Stablecoin backed by ETH with algorithmic stability mechanisms.
- [sUSD](https://synthetix.io/) — Synthetic stablecoin within the Synthetix ecosystem.
- [FRAX](https://frax.finance/) — Fractional-algorithmic stablecoin combining collateral and algorithmic mechanisms.

## Algorithmic Stablecoins

Stablecoins that rely on supply-demand mechanisms rather than full collateralization.

- [Ampleforth (AMPL)](https://www.ampleforth.org/) — Elastic supply token designed to maintain price stability.
- [Fei Protocol (FEI)](https://fei.money/) — Algorithmic stablecoin using direct incentives for peg stability.
- [Terra (UST - legacy)](https://terra.money/) — Algorithmic model demonstrating risks of under-collateralized systems.

## Stablecoin Infrastructure

Core platforms and services enabling issuance, custody, and integration.

- [Circle](https://www.circle.com/) — Infrastructure for issuing and managing USDC.
- [Paxos](https://paxos.com/) — Regulated blockchain infrastructure for stablecoins and tokenization.
- [Fireblocks](https://www.fireblocks.com/) — Digital asset custody and transfer infrastructure.
- [BitGo](https://www.bitgo.com/) — Custody, wallet, and financial services infrastructure.
- [Wyre](https://www.sendwyre.com/) — Fiat-to-crypto infrastructure and payment APIs.

## Payments & Integrations

Tools and platforms for using stablecoins in real-world transactions.

- [Stripe Crypto](https://stripe.com/crypto) — Payment infrastructure supporting stablecoin transactions.
- [Coinbase Commerce](https://commerce.coinbase.com/) — Merchant platform for accepting crypto and stablecoins.
- [BitPay](https://bitpay.com/) — Payment processor supporting stablecoin transactions and cards.
- [MoonPay](https://www.moonpay.com/) — On-ramp and off-ramp infrastructure for fiat and crypto.
- [Ramp](https://ramp.network/) — Payment infrastructure connecting fiat and stablecoins.
- [LemonCake](https://lemoncake.xyz?utm_source=awesome-stablecoins&utm_medium=github) — USDC Pay Tokens for AI agents, with hard spending caps, sub-second kill-switch, and automatic journaling into freee / Money Forward / QuickBooks.

## Analytics & Data

Platforms for tracking stablecoin supply, flows, and market behavior.

- [Glassnode](https://glassnode.com/) — On-chain analytics platform for tracking stablecoin flows and metrics.
- [Nansen](https://www.nansen.ai/) — Blockchain analytics and wallet intelligence platform.
- [Dune](https://dune.com/) — Query-based analytics platform for blockchain data.
- [Token Terminal](https://tokenterminal.com/) — Financial metrics and analytics for crypto assets.
- [CoinGecko](https://www.coingecko.com/) — Market data and stablecoin tracking.

## Regulation & Compliance

Frameworks and resources related to stablecoin governance and oversight.

- [MiCA (EU Regulation)](https://finance.ec.europa.eu/capital-markets-union-and-financial-markets/markets-crypto-assets-regulation-mica_en) — European regulatory framework for crypto assets and stablecoins.
- [New York DFS Stablecoin Guidance](https://www.dfs.ny.gov/) — Regulatory guidance for stablecoin issuers.
- [Financial Stability Board (FSB)](https://www.fsb.org/) — Global policy recommendations for stablecoins.
- [Bank for International Settlements (BIS)](https://www.bis.org/) — Research and policy work on digital currencies.
